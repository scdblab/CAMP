Dr. Shahram Ghandeharizadeh (Founder and Interim CEO) is the inventor of KOSAR as the flagship product offering of Mitra. He is recognized for his pioneering contributions to the design and implementation of scalable, highly available, and elastic data stores for more than quarter a century. This includes benchmarks such as BG to characterize system performance. His activities have been recognized by numerous awards including the prestigious 2008 ACM Software System award. Dr. Ghandeharizadeh is on the faculty of the University of Southern California where he directs the USC Database Laboratory.

Adam Carney (Director of Sales and Business Development) is a web entrepreneur in Los Angeles. Before working at Kosar he was an entrepreneur in residence at Science-Inc., an early stage investment firm in Santa Monica, and founded LittleQuest.com, the largest online resource for youth programs in the world. He currently consults for many companies in the area of online marketing and business development, including Petsafe.com (Fortune 500).

Shahram Mehraban (Director of Marketing) provides more than 20 years of experience marketing innovative software and hardware solutions, ranging from chip design to embedded energy efficient (IoT) devices. He is responsible for setting research directions, conducting comparative qualitative and quantitative research globally, and developing new strategy for future growth. Mehraban holds a Master of Business Administration degree from the University of Victoria. He is a self-proclaimed tech enthusiast and an early adopter of all types of technology. He likes to run and watch European soccer during the weekends.
